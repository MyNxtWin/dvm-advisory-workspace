Pasta Aglio e Olio
==================
Serves 2 | Ready in 20 minutes

Ingredients
-----------
200g spaghetti
6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
4 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp red chilli flakes
A handful of fresh parsley, chopped
Salt & black pepper
Parmesan to serve (optional)

Steps
-----
1. Boil salted water and cook spaghetti until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan on medium-low.
3. Add garlic slices — cook gently until golden (not brown), about 3 minutes.
4. Add chilli flakes, stir for 30 seconds.
5. Add drained pasta to the pan with a splash of pasta water. Toss well.
6. Add parsley, season with salt and pepper, toss again.
7. Serve immediately with parmesan if desired.

Tips
----
- Don't rush the garlic — low and slow is the secret.
- Pasta water is starchy and makes the sauce silky; don't skip it.
